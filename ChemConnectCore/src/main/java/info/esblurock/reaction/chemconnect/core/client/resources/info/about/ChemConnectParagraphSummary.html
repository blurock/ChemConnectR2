CHEMCONNECT

CHEMCONNECT goes beyond traditional data repositories in that the data is parsed, using a laboratory and device specific protocol (template) description, and analyzed with respect to an extensive chemical and combustion knowledge base within CHEMCONNECT. The parsed data is then linked allowing for efficient searching and comparison of combustion data. The goal is to report all data associated with experiments, from a device description, to device calibrations, the intermediate data (both computed and measured), to the final published results. Associated with the data are procedure and methodology descriptions and associated links and references. Having published data linked to its dependent measurements and constants, devices, subsystems, sensors and even people and laboratories provides an effective accountability and more confidence in the data. Data entry and availability can range from private user, to user defined consortia to general public.
